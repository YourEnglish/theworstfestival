DUNE RATS (AUS) are masters in creating songs that bring you sheer joy and the pleasures of an ecstatic crowd. To be sure, these 3 hyperactive stoner cunts from Brisbane didn't come here for a hair cut. Vocalist/guitarist Danny Beus, drummer BC Michaels & bassist Brett Jansch are the inventors of DUNECORE stoner pop, an energetic and happy mix of indie/jangle/surf/garage rock. Never shy of a fine prank they remain the crowd pleasers you have been waiting for.
"Dalai Lama, Big Banana, Marijuana..."
